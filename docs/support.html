Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync data between parents?

Enable iCloud Sync in Settings, then tap "Invite Partner". This generates an iCloud sharing link that your partner can open to access the same baby data.

Can I track multiple babies?

Yes! Tap your baby's profile photo in the dashboard or go to Settings to switch between babies. You can add as many profiles as you need.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings > Language > App Language. You can choose between English, French, and Finnish. The change takes effect immediately.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account. We have no servers and no access to your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. You can export in CSV, XML, or a full readable report format.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Delete All Data. This permanently removes all activities, growth records, and health records for the selected baby.
